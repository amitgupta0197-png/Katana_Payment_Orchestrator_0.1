KATANA PAY

Katana Pay is a payment orchestration platform for UPI-first businesses — hosted checkout, real-time reconciliation, smart routing and fast settlements behind one signed API.

Who we partner with:

  • Merchants accepting payments on UPI, cards, net banking and wallets.
  • Banking partners providing collection, settlement and escrow rails.
  • Technology providers embedding fully-branded, white-label payments.
  • Payment partners plugging PSPs, gateways and acquiring banks into the network.

What you get:

  • Recurring revenue for every merchant you refer to Katana Pay.
  • Smart multi-provider routing scored on success rate, latency, health and cost.
  • Real-time reconciliation with RRN/UTR tracking on every credit.
  • Fast upline → Katana → branch settlements with immutable timelines.
